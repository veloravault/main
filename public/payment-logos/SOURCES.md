# Payment logo sources

These files are unmodified downloaded brand marks. They are displayed only to identify supported payment methods.

- `visa.svg`: current Visa mark, downloaded via Wikimedia Commons from `File:Visa Inc. logo (2021-present).svg`; authoritative usage guidance: https://corporate.visa.com/en/about-visa/brand.html
- `mastercard.svg`: current Mastercard symbol, downloaded via Wikimedia Commons from `File:Mastercard 2019 logo.svg`; authoritative usage guidance: https://www.mastercard.com/brandcenter/us/en/download-artwork.html
- `rupay.svg`: RuPay mark, downloaded via Wikimedia Commons from `File:RuPay.svg`; authoritative assets and guidance: https://www.npci.org.in/digital-media-kit
- `upi.svg`: UPI mark, downloaded via Wikimedia Commons from `File:UPI-Logo-vector.svg`; the Government of India presentation portal also publishes the mark at https://www.presentations.gov.in/logos/unified-payments-interface-upi/

Do not redraw, recolor, distort, or convert these marks into JSX/CSS artwork.
